ACBSP Releases Volume 3 of its Peer-Reviewed Journal and Issues a Call for Papers for Volume 4

The Transnational Journal of Business, Vol. 3 is now available in print ($20, including S&H) or as a free digital download. The five articles featured went through a rigorous, double-blind peer review process prior to final acceptance for publication.

The Scholar-Practitioner Publications Committee of the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) has issued a Call for Papers for The Transnational Journal of Business, Vol. 4. The deadline to submit articles is December 15!

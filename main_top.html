Nathan Eberline Appointed Vice President of Operations at ACBSP

The Board of Directors of ACBSP announces that Nathan Eberline, J.D. has been appointed Vice President of Operations for the organization effective May 7, 2018. “Nathan brings a wealth of knowledge and varied experience in the nonprofit, legal, and governmental industries to the organization, said President & CEO Jeffrey Alderman. “His unique skillset is an ideal fit as we continue to grow ACBSP throughout the world.”
